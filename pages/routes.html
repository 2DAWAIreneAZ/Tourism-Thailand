Curated itineraries to experience the best of Thailand's culture, history, and nature.

The Classic Route

A 6-Day Journey from Capital to Coast

Day 1-2

Bangkok

2 Days Culture & City

Start in Thailand’s capital. Visit the Grand Palace, Wat Phra Kaew, Wat Pho, and Wat Arun. Explore local markets, take a boat ride along the Chao Phraya River, and enjoy famous street food.

Grand Palace Wat Arun Street Food
Day 3

Ayutthaya

1 Day Train/Bus from BKK

Explore the ancient capital of the Kingdom of Siam. Visit impressive temple ruins and historical parks reflecting Thailand’s past. A calm contrast to Bangkok's energy.

Temple Ruins History Park
Day 4

Chiang Mai

1 Day North & Culture

Head north for traditional atmosphere. Visit historic temples in the old city, walk through local markets, and experience Northern Thai culture.

Old City Night Bazaar
Day 5-6

Krabi or Phuket

2 Days Nature & Relax

End the journey in southern Thailand. Spend time on tropical beaches, explore islands, snorkel, or take boat tours. A peaceful conclusion to the trip.

Beaches Island Hopping
